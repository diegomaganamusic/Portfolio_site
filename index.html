About

What do you want to know about me? I'm just a dude from Oxnard, CA who makes music and releases it so that I feel like I've left a permanent mark on the world before I die and turn back to dust. In my freetime I like to garden or devote time to my non-profit MiniNature Reserve. I also love hiking, working out, and swimming. If you're curious about my music, let me first warn you that it can be very eclectic. I like my music like I like my food. Savory, sweet, spicy, creamy, and acidic all in one dish (album). Like a Thai Curry! Listen at your own risk!
